About Linda Osaghale

Linda Osaghale is a Microbiologist, Bioinformatician, and AI/ML Researcher with a First‑Class Master's degree in Environmental Microbiology from the University of Ibadan, where she was recognized as the Best Graduating Student. She founded LD WORLD GLOBAL to empower researchers, students, and organisations to achieve excellence in their academic and research journeys.

She has published as first/corresponding author in multiple Scopus‑indexed journals. She is known for her high level of creativity and deep expertise in research, data analytics, and academic mentoring.
